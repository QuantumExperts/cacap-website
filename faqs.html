Frequently Asked Questions

FAQs

Which process provides a binding and enforceable decision?
Arbitration provides a private adjudication process that results in a binding and enforceable award. Learn about arbitration →
How does mediation help parties resolve construction disputes?
Mediation is a confidential and collaborative process where a neutral mediator facilitates discussions between parties to help them reach a voluntary and mutually acceptable settlement agreement. Learn about mediation →
Which approach is used for disputes involving technical expertise?
Expert Determination is ideal for technical and specialist disputes. A neutral expert with relevant expertise assesses the issues and provides a binding determination based on their specialised knowledge. Learn about expert determination →
Who offers impartial technical advice during formal proceedings?
Expert Witness services provide independent analysis, reports, and testimony to assist tribunals, courts, or arbitrators in understanding complex technical or specialised matters. Learn about expert witnesses →
What functions do Dispute Boards serve?
Dispute Boards provide proactive oversight, guidance, and timely recommendations or decisions on construction projects to prevent disputes, resolve issues efficiently, and maintain project relationships. Learn about dispute boards →
What happens if parties cannot agree on the appointment of a neutral?
CACAP provides nomination services to appoint a suitably qualified and impartial professional in accordance with the needs of the case and applicable contract provisions. Learn about nomination services →
