Nomination Request Form

We offer a confidential and independent nomination service to help you appoint the right dispute resolver for your situation. Whether you need support for arbitration, mediation, expert determination, adjudication, dispute boards, or expert witness services, we are here to assist you in identifying a qualified neutral who fits the needs of your dispute.

How the Nomination Process Works
  1. Parties submit a request providing details of the dispute and the expertise required.
  2. CACAP reviews its panel to identify the most suitable candidate.
  3. Independence and absence of conflicts of interest are confirmed.
  4. The nominated neutral is shared with the parties for agreement.
  5. Once agreed, the neutral is formally appointed to proceed with the matter.
