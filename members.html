Our Representatives

CACAP Country Representatives

The Centre's Country Representatives represent the diversity of construction and infrastructure expertise across the Asia Pacific region. Our representatives bring extensive experience in dispute resolution, construction law, engineering, and project management. Together, they provide guidance on the Centre's operations, appointment procedures, and accreditation standards.
