Why CACAP

Independent. Specialist. Asia Pacific.

The Construction Arbitration Centre (Asia Pacific) (CACAP) is a centre of training, appointment and nomination of qualified neutrals for construction and infrastructure disputes in the Asia-Pacific region. CACAP facilitates the appointment of experts for arbitration, mediation, expert determination, dispute boards and related dispute resolution processes.

Expert Appointments
