Membership Categories

Membership with the Construction Arbitration Centre (CACAP) is open to qualified professionals involved in construction dispute resolution. Members form our rosters of neutrals who may be considered for appointments across Asia Pacific disputes.

Arbitrator
Mediator
Expert Determiner
Expert Witness
Dispute Board
Adjudicator

Benefits of Membership

  • Eligibility for inclusion on CACAP’s panel of neutrals and consideration for nomination or appointment.
  • Listing in CACAP’s professional roster of arbitrators, mediators, expert determiners, adjudicators, and dispute board members.
  • Access to member updates, panel opportunities, and notification of nomination requests.
  • Priority access to CACAP professional development programmes, training, and networking events.
  • Opportunity to serve as an appointed neutral in disputes throughout the Asia Pacific region.
  • Members may also be eligible for CACAP professional accreditation upon successful completion of a CACAP training course. View the Accreditation Scheme.

Application Process

  1. Review and Apply: Review the eligibility requirements and click Apply for Membership below or visit the application form.
  2. Complete Application: Provide all required information regarding your professional qualifications and experience.
  3. Panel Committee Review: Your application will be reviewed by the Panel Selection Committee (typically 5–7 business days).
  4. Approval Notification: Upon acceptance, a notification will be sent to your email address.
  5. Profile Management: Access the Member Portal to manage your profile, availability, and credentials.
