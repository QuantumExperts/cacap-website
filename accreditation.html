Accreditation Programme

About the CACAP Accreditation Scheme

The Construction Arbitration Centre Asia Pacific operates a professional accreditation programme for dispute resolution practitioners across the Asia Pacific region. Accreditation is awarded to individuals who have successfully completed a CACAP training course and who have demonstrated, through assessed practical sessions, an exemplary standard of competence, understanding, and professional aptitude in their respective discipline.

Accreditation Disciplines

Dispute Board

Dispute Board Practice

CACAP Accredited Dispute Board Practitioner

Recognises practical competence in the constitution, operation, and procedures of dispute boards, including dispute adjudication boards, dispute review boards, and combined dispute boards.

Arbitration

Arbitration

CACAP Accredited Arbitrator

Recognises practical competence in arbitral procedure, the conduct of hearings, the assessment of evidence, and the preparation of reasoned awards.

Expert Determination

Expert Determination

CACAP Accredited Expert Determiner

Recognises practical competence in the conduct of expert determination proceedings, including the evaluation of submissions and the preparation of binding determinations.

Mediation

Mediation

CACAP Accredited Mediator

Recognises practical competence in mediation techniques, facilitated negotiation, and the structured resolution of construction and commercial disputes.

Adjudication

Adjudication

CACAP Accredited Adjudicator

Recognises practical competence in statutory and contractual adjudication procedures, including the rapid assessment of claims within compressed timeframes.

Basis of Award

Accreditation is not awarded automatically upon completion of a training course. It is reserved for those candidates who, in the assessment of the course instructors and with the approval of the President of CACAP, have demonstrated an exceptional standard of understanding, practical application, and professional aptitude during the assessed practical sessions. Each certificate bears a unique reference number and records the specific training course upon which the accreditation is founded.
